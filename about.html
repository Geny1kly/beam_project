Welcome to Steam

What is Steam?

Steam is the ultimate destination for playing, discussing, and creating games. It offers thousands of games from AAA to indie and everything in between. Enjoy exclusive deals, automatic game updates, and other great perks.

Why you'll love it

Instant Access

Join the Steam community and access thousands of games instantly, no physical media required.

Automatic Updates

All your games stay up to date with the latest patches and updates delivered automatically.

Multiplayer & Chat

Play with friends or match with new players in multiplayer games, with integrated chat and groups.

Cloud Saves

Pick up where you left off with cloud saving for your favorite titles, on any machine.

Available on Multiple Platforms

Steam works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. It also powers the Steam Deck, a powerful handheld gaming device to play your library on the go.

Join Over 100 Billion Users

With over 100 billion users worldwide, Steam is one of the largest gaming communities in existence. Discover, play, and contribute to the gaming world.
